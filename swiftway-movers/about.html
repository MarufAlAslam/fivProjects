About us

Moving can be one of life's most stressful events so we’re here to help you manage your relocation stress.
Sound impossible? It’s not. 96% of our customers would refer us to friends and family for their next move.

Proud Member of United Van Lines

With more than 125 years in industry, Canada Moving offers professional household moving and commercial moving for local moves; with 24 local moving branches and provides long distance moving through United Van Lines and Mayflower Canada’s moving network consisting of more than 165 moving partners Worldwide.

Canada Moving also works with MoverOne International , servicing cross-border moves (U.S.), including international moves in +160 countries worldwide.

Our Mission

At Canada Moving, our top priority is to provide a positive moving experience for our customers whether moving across the street or around the world . We can help make your next move trouble-free.

Some Quick Facts About Canada Moving

  • Largest Family Owned Canadian Moving Company
  • Established in 1890 in London Ontario
  • Number of Vehicles Worldwide: 17,700
  • More than 800 Staff
  • Warehouse Space Worldwide: 39 Million sq. ft.
  • Annual Shipments: 48,000
  • Countries Moved to Annually: 184
